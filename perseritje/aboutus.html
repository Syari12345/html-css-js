What Is Digital School?

Digital School (from KOSOVO) is a private company for the training and education of children and teens (8-18 years old) in technology and programming.

With the motto “Improvise CODE Overcome” the vision of the School is to equip your children and teenagers with the skills necessary to succeed in today's world, where every profession is related to digital skills.

The main focus of the Digital School is on teaching the logic of programming in a way that programmers will use young people in the other language program they will use in the future.

While students learn the programming skills of those who can develop software products such as applications and websites , if they learn to critique skills and abilities and solve rapid trends in technology and in general life.

Digital School is the first and only company in Kosovo which has become a Franchise for the retail and international market. Digital School franchises today operate with several locations in Slovenia, Germany, USA, Macedonia, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Mexico, Hong Kong, Morocco and Tunisia.

Digital School is a course accredited by the National Qualifications Authority, meaning that students get accredited certificates with the title "Junior Programmer".
